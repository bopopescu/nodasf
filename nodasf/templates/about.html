About NodaSF

NodaSF is part of the NodaNews Network and provides news and information about San Francisco and the Bay Area. I believe that the news media is failing, and we need to revisit this business model.
NodaSF contains no advertisements, and all revenue comes from joint ventures with local people and donations. News companies should be embedded in the society they operate. We need to destroy the corporate news establishment that peddles partisan news and politicized “hot takes” that divide us.
All of that is bullshit, and NodaSF is one step toward a more reasonable media culture. Philippe Morel sf@nodanews.com
